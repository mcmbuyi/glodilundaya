KongoLisolo Foundation in the acronym «KLF» is to help kids and the families in need arround the world.We want ensure that every kid and families gets some kind of education and that noneof them goes to the bed without eating something. We are part of KongoLisolo branch.
Mission
Our mission is to enrich life of children and families on need... We inspire them to dream and achive their goals, regardless of race,color creed, religion, or economic status. We also strive to make a lasting difference by providing opportunities for education, food and much more.
Goals
  • Provide jobs
  • To provide food and water
  • To spread the power of optimism
  • To provide education to kids and families
  • Building a world where life is good for all
  • To provide acces to medication and health services
  • Encourage community action that supports those in need
Why we do it
Because right now life isn't good for all people.
